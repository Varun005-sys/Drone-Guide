Drone Equipment

Drone Frame

The structure that holds all drone components together.

Drone Motors

Spin the propellers to generate thrust and lift.

Drone Propellers

Rotating blades that allow the drone to fly.

Electronic Speed Controller

Regulates the speed of the motors by controlling power.

Flight Controller

The brain of the drone that stabilizes and controls flight.

Li-Po Battery

Provides power to the drone and all components.

GPS Module

Helps drones navigate and return home safely.

Transmitter

The remote controller used by the pilot to fly the drone.

Receiver

Receives signals from the transmitter for controlling the drone.

Drone Camera

Captures photos and videos during drone flight.

Camera Gimbal

Stabilizes the camera to capture smooth video footage.

Landing Gear

Supports the drone when landing or taking off.

Power Distribution Board

Distributes power from the battery to all drone components.

FPV Camera

Provides first-person view video to the pilot in real-time.

Video Transmitter

Sends live video from the drone to the ground station.

Drone Antenna

Improves communication between drone and pilot.

Compass Sensor

Helps determine the orientation of the drone in flight.

Barometer Sensor

Measures altitude and helps stabilize drone at a fixed height.

Obstacle Avoidance Sensor

Detects obstacles and prevents collisions.

LED Lights

Helps with drone visibility and orientation during flight.
